What We Believe

Here at the Seattle Church of Christ we believe that we are called to live out the teachings of the New Testament in fellowship with one another. We believe that Jesus Christ is Lord and that we are called to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son and Holy Spirit and teaching them to obey everything that we've been commanded. Our shared faith with our brothers and sisters in Christ should show the world the love and light of Jesus' ministry.
